About dataset¶

IMPORTANT: The dataset is curated by scraping publically available job listings on Upwork.com. The data is a sample of about ~9500 jobs listed on upwork.com which made up ~6.25% of total jobs available on upwork at the time of scraping.

the dataset can be found at https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/hashiromer/upwork-jobs
